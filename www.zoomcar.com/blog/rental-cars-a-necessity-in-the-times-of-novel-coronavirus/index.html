Rental Cars: A Necessity in the Times of Novel Coronavirus

By Zoomcar Admin August 24, 2020 4 Mins Read
8,302
Share
Share on Facebook Share on Twitter LinkedIn Email

The outbreak and the subsequent spread of the novel Coronavirus have led to challenges that have never been seen before. Amidst this pandemic and the enforced lockdowns, the personal mobility of individuals has also been affected adversely.

Many people are not in a position to buy a car during these difficult times due to job losses and budget cuts. Moreover, people during this phase are also inclined to spend money only on essential purchases. Using public transport is also out of question due to the increased risk of virus transmission it poses. Hence, this leaves only one option for safe mobility for individuals – long term car rentals.

Renting cars from car rental companies during these pandemic times offer several advantages, let us take a look at them:

  • Enables you to Follow Social Distancing Norms

It is obvious that social distancing cannot be followed properly while using public transport. Travelling in public transport without any social distancing will make you more susceptible to contracting the virus. With social distancing being considered the need of the hour, it is vital to have some means of private travelling for safe travelling. It can be done easily through monthly car rentals, that we at Zoomcar offer at affordable rates. Having a car during such tough times will allow you to maintain social distancing and travel safely.

  • Enables you to Travel with Precautions

The best car rental services are taking the utmost precautions to provide you with safe travelling experiences. We at Zoomcar provide car pick-up and drop facilities for consumers, so that you do not have to travel to rent a car. Apart from this, we have also implemented strict hygiene and safety measures such as:

  • Each part of our cars, including the seats, door and boot handles, and gears, are thoroughly sanitized before being delivered to you.
  • To reduce physical contact, we have leveraged best-in-class new-age technologies for implementing keyless entry (KLE) in our cars, which will allow you to lock or unlock your rented car using the Zoomcar app.
  • We are also conducting extensive training sessions for our employees to inform them about standard measures such as wearing face masks, washing hands for 20 seconds, and social distancing to avoid contracting the virus.

Such hygiene and safety practices will enable you to take up an option of long-term car rental without any stress and allow you to travel with stringent precautionary measures in place.

  • Enables you to Travel Anywhere

Monthly car rentals from Zoomcar come equipped with all-India permits, thus allowing you to visit travel anywhere in India with your rented car, whether it’s heading back to your home town or a simple road trip. Apart from this, we also offer 24×7 roadside assistance. If you have car troubles, contact us at Zoomcar, and we will support you by taking away your car for repairs and providing you with a replacement car for that duration. To provide you with the best customer service, Zoomcar also makes use of emails, text messages, calls, and chatbots to answer your queries and doubts 24×7.

Freedom to Travel Freely with Zoomcar

Zoomcar also offers other car rental benefits such as insurance and accident coverage and no down payment. Renting a car provides you with the benefits of owning a car, but without the financial expenses of loans, insurance premiums, registration, and taxes. It also gives you the benefit of personal mobility, allowing you to travel when and where you want without the risk of contracting the virus.  

We, at Zoomcar, offer attractive and affordable long term car rental and monthly car rental packages in cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Chandigarh for safe travelling. You even get to choose from car types like hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, and luxury for your rental.

So, what are you waiting for? Book a Zoomcar rental and visit your parents in your home town or go on that road trip that you have been planning for a long time!
